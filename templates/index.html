How to Use ?

Our website is user friendly and easy to use.Please follow the steps if you get confused.

  • To become a user,one must sign-up on our website.
  • After successful login,you can find your mail-id at top right corner which contains a drop down
  • Choose select preferences to select/change your preferences.
  • Select your desired newspaper for the given categories and select your mode of message.
  • Choose my preferences to check your preferences.
  • That's it, you will receive a mail/sms from us which contains headlines of your preffered choices.
  • You can click on the headline in the message to read the full article about that particular headline.
